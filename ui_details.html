Welcome to the Constitution of India Basics

This website helps you learn the fundamentals of the Indian Constitution in simple language.

Key Principles of the Indian Constitution

Preamble

The Preamble is the introduction to the Constitution, stating the guiding principles of the nation.

Fundamental Rights

Fundamental Rights are the basic human rights guaranteed to all citizens of India.

Directive Principles

These are guidelines for the state to ensure social and economic welfare.
